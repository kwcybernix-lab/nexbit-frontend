How it works
1
Select network, enter amount & phone
Choose your network, the phone number the airtime is on, and the amount you want to sell. Minimum is ₦100.
2
Receive your Nexbit transfer number
After clicking "Sell airtime", a dedicated Nexbit number appears. This is the number you will send the airtime to.
3
Transfer the airtime via USSD
Use your network's transfer code (e.g. MTN: *321*[number]*[amount]*[pin]#) to send the airtime to the Nexbit number.

Take a clear screenshot of the successful transfer confirmation from your phone and upload it as proof.
5
Get paid within 30 minutes
Our team reviews your screenshot and credits your Nexbit wallet or bank account within 15–30 minutes.
